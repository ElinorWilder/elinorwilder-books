About Me

Elinor Wilder lives on a quiet mountain where the trees are tall, the wind is sharp,
and the sea is always somewhere far below.

She shares her cabin with four unruly dogs and one cat who does not believe he lives there—but you know how cats are.

She collects feathers, bones, and bits of weathered wood, turning them into art no one ever sees.

When she’s not writing emotionally intense wilderness romances, she’s probably outside, pretending
she’s not waiting for wolves.
